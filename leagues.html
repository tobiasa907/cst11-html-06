    football league is an association of football clubs that organizes matches between member teams of a similar standard. Usually teams will play each other in a round robin set up, points are awarded, 3 points for a win, 1 point for a draw and 0 points for a lose. They are governed by set of rules decided by founding clubs, usually in line with country FA rules, then with FIFA rules. Examples of leagues English Premier League La Liga French League 1. League uses a table to indicate the stats at any time , to keep record of games played , points etc. After all games are played, that is each team has played against everyone, twice, (home and away) , the team with highest number of points is the winner of the league for that season, a season is usually a year long. The team at the bottom, with the lowest number of points, is relegated from the league and a new team joins. In the event there is a tie(i.e 2 or more teams with equal number of points) there are different ways to break the tie, depending on the rules of that league; Examples, which can be in any order depending on the league; Look at the team with the highest goal difference. team with the highest GF , then lowest GA. Results of head to head between the 2 teams tied. A final match played to determine winner. Toss a coin or pull a name out of the hat, literally.
